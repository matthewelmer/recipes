Egg Fried Rice
==============

Servings: 1

Ingredients:
- 2-3 cloves of garlic
- A shallot(?)
- 2 egg
- Day old rice
- Spring onion
- Fresh chili (chopped)
- Sambal
- Soy sauce
- Vegetable oil (or refined sesame oil?)
- Pork fat
- Toasted sesame oil
- MSG
- White pepper

Instructions:
1. Heat oil till smokey (wok hay)
2. Garlic and shallot
3. Stir fry
4. Egg
5. Stir fry
6. Rice
7. Stir fry
8. Soy sauce
9. Toasted sesame oil
10. Stir fry
11. Flatten rice so no clumps
12. MSG
13. White pepper
14. Stir fry
15. Sambal
16. Spring onion and chili
17. Toss
18. Eat with spoon
